1) Introduction¶

In this homework, time series analysis of:

- General economic situation expectation over the next 12 months 
- USD-TL exchange rate
- CPI level  
- Total industrial production

is investigated using the monthly data of the 2012 Jan - 2021 Dec period. The aim is to understand if these datas have a similiar pattern or correlation or in other words, can we utilize one data to understand or predict the other one. After investigating the EVDS data, I will try to relate search volume (in Turkey) of the following words:

-Dollar (Dolar)
-Job Postings Abroad (Yurtd  lanlar)
-Inflation (Enflasyon)

To understand if there is a relationship between the data level and the search volume

5) Conclusions.¶

The aim of this report was analyzing the relationship of chosen datasets with time and search volume of the keywords. Conclusions are:

CPI level and TL-USD exchange rate is highly positively correlated and General economic situation expectation over the next 12 months is highly negatively correlated with these two variables.

Total production is in an increasing trend and it peaks at the end of the year, bottoms at the beginning of the year.

Search volume of "enflasyon" and "dolar" is highly correlated with change in exchange rate and CPI level

There is an increasing trend in the search volume of "yurtd i ilanlar" which is highly negatively correlated with worsening general economic situtation expectations.
